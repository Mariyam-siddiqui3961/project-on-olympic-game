Olympic Games

There are several types of Olympics, including -
  • Summer Olympics
  • Winter Olympics
  • Paralympics
  • YOG

1. Summer Olympics

One of the biggest sporting events in the world, where athletes compete for gold, silver, and bronze medals in individual and team sports.

2. Winter Olympics

Held every four years, alternating with the Summer Olympics every two years since 1994

3. Paralympics

Features athletes with impairments

4. Youth Olympic Games

Features competitors between the ages of 15 and 18
